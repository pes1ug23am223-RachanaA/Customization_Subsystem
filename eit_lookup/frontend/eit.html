EIT Handler

Extra Information Types — define custom structured fields for employees, jobs, and positions

Connecting...
EITs capture additional structured data beyond standard HR fields. Each EIT is linked to a context (EMPLOYEE, JOB, or POSITION) and integrates with the Recruitment and Onboarding subsystems. EITs can be injected into forms — select an EIT and use the "Add to Form" button to embed it.
Note: Employee and candidate records from external subsystems are read-only here.
